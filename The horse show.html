The horse show is full of silver and gold medals. The horse stalls are marked with the ribbons, they are pined on the metal grates. She speaks fast and never slows down to let us tell her we barely have a horse, only a pony.

It's a service I offer. I have a feeling you're grieving someone right now, someone ran away?

Mandy watches the bottle rolling in her hand. Yes, my cat, Sofie. She's been missing for over a year now.

Mandy meets the woman's eyes and gives her a light smile. The woman continues, I can see that you have been grieving intensely. Perhaps that is because Sofie is trying to speak to you, and once you can talk to each other, the pain will be eased.

Mandy nods, I know something bad happened, I just wish I knew what. Like, if it was something I did that was preventable.

Mandy puts the bottle back on the table and looks towards me, I try and move my head to leave without letting the woman see. It works, and we thank her one after the other. She looks down at us with knowing eyes and says she'll be there the whole show, in case you have more questions.
